# 📊 Ferns and Petals Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

## 🚀 Project Overview
The **Ferns and Petals Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Excel-based business intelligence project designed to analyze sales data and generate actionable insights.  

This dashboard helps understand business performance across multiple dimensions such as time, product categories, customer behavior, and geographic locations.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze sales data to identify trends and patterns  
- Track key business metrics (revenue, orders, delivery time)  
- Identify top-performing products and categories  
- Understand customer behavior across occasions  
- Enable data-driven decision making  

---

## 📌 Key Metrics
- **Total Orders:** 1000  
- **Total Revenue:** 3.52 Million  
- **Average Delivery Time:** 5.53 days  
- **Average Customer Spending:** 3520.98  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 1. Monthly Sales Trends
- Peak sales in **February** and **August**
- Low sales in **April, July, September**
- Shows strong seasonal patterns

### 2. Revenue by Category
- Top categories: **Colors, Soft Toys**
- Low categories: **Mugs, Plants**

### 3. Top 5 Products
- Magnam Set  
- Quia Gift  
- Dolores Gift  
- Harum Pack  
- Deserunt Box  

### 4. Revenue by Cities
- High-performing cities: **Imphal, Dhanbad, Kavali**
- Opportunity to grow in other regions

### 5. Occasion-Based Analysis
- High revenue: **Anniversary, Raksha Bandhan**
- Low revenue: **Diwali, Valentine’s Day**

### 6. Revenue by Time
- Peak hours: **5 PM – 8 PM**
- Low activity: Morning hours
